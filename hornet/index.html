Hornet, also known as the Gendered Child or the Daughter of Hallownest, is the daughter of the Pale King in an official affair with Herrah the Beast. Herrah agreed on becoming a dreamer if she would be given a child. And she was given Hornet. Hornet wears a red cloth, and she is one of the only citizens of Hallownest who has been outside of Hallownest and come back with intact mind. She helps the Ghost throughout their adventure in discovering Hallownest, testing their worth
